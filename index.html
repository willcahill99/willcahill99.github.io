About

Editor, producer,
problem solver

I'm a producer and editor based in Los Angeles with a dual B.S. in Television Radio Film and Information Management & Technology from Syracuse University's Renée Crown Honors Program. That blend of creative storytelling and technical precision shapes everything I do in the edit.

Currently a Production Specialist at X-15 Industries, I oversee quality control and final delivery of digital, broadcast, and social spots while collaborating across production and editorial teams. On the freelance side, I edit podcasts, color grade music videos, and independently produce short films — always looking for the next compelling story to tell.

When I'm not cutting, you'll find me behind a camera. Photography keeps my visual eye sharp and feeds back into my editorial work — how I frame, pace, and feel a story.
